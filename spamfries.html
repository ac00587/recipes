Air-Fried SPAM Fries

by Rob Kramlich

Ingredients

  • 1 (12-ounce) can SPAM Classic
  • Peanut oil, for frying
  • Sriracha Mayo
  • 1 tbsp Tony Chachere's Seasoning

Directions

  1. Cut SPAM Classic into 6 slices. Cut each slice into 4 thick matchsticks.
  2. Place fries into air fryer basket and cook at 360Â°F 12 to 15 minutes or until desired crispiness.
  3. While the fries are in the air fryer, mix the Sriracha Mayo and Tony Chachere's Seasoning in small bowl.
  4. Serve the warm fries with dipping sauces.
  5. Enjoy!
