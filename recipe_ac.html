Roasted Asparagus

by Anthony Chatfield

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of thin asparagus spears, trimmed
  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced

Directions

Preheat oven to 425. Place everything in a bow except the asparagus and mix. Toss asparagus in bowl. Seperate asparagus on a baking sheet so that none overlap. Bake 15 minutes.
