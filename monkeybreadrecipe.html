Monkey Bread Recipe

by Lindsey Moore

Ingredients

  • 30 oz of refrigerated biscuit dough
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespons Cinnamon
  • 1 stick of melted butter

Directions

Step 1: Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. Coat a bundt pan with a layer of non-stick cooking spray and set aside Step 2: Cut each bisuit into quarters, toss in the cinnamon sugar mixture and place in a greased bundt pan. Step 3: Combine melted butter and brown sugar pour the butter mixture over the bisuit pieces in the pan. It will look like to much but it this is what makes it amazing. Step 4: Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the top is golden brown and the center is cooked through. Remove from the oven and allow to cool for 10 minutes before turning over onto a plate.
